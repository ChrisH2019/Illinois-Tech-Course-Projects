  • Jewel Osco

    © jewelosco.com

    This is your typical grocery store, there are 2 near campus. One is walking distance straight down 34th street to Martin Luther King Drive and the other is right off the Roosevelt stop on the Red and Green Lines. Be cautious when going to either of these at night. If you must get groceries at night, do not go by yourself.
